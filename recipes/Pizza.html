Homemade Pizza

Description

This easy pizza dough recipe is great for beginners and produces 2 soft and chewy homemade pizza crusts. Skip the pizza delivery because you only need 6 basic ingredients to begin!

Ingredients

  • Yeast
  • Sugar
  • Tomato sauce
  • Flour
  • Water
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Prep the Dough: Mix yeast, warm water, sugar; let activate, then add flour, salt, knead until smooth, and let rise for 1-2 hours.
  2. Prepare the Toppings: Get tomato sauce ready, grate mozzarella, and prep other toppings (pepperoni, veggies, herbs).
  3. Shape the Dough: Punch down the dough, divide, and roll/stretch into your desired pizza shape.
  4. Assemble the Pizza: Spread sauce, add cheese, and layer on your toppings.
  5. Bake: Preheat oven to 475°F (245°C), bake for 10-15 minutes until crust is golden and cheese is bubbly.
  6. Finish & Serve: Add fresh basil or olive oil, slice, and enjoy!
